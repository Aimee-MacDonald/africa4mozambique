Request for Relief Aid for Areas in Mozambique Affected by Cyclone Idai

This document serves as a formal request for relief aid and assistance for areas affected by the recent cyclone that devastated parts of Mozambique, by Africa for Mozambique, a task force commisioned by Global Pan Africanism Network in collaboration with Association of University Students of the University of Eduardo Mondlane (AEU-UEM).

The nature of the collaboration sees Africa for Mozambique sourcing and forwarding relief aid and expertise from the various regions of the globe, where it is operational, and handing over the raised relief aid to AEU-UEM., which is on the ground in Mozambique, for delivery and implementation in disaster areas. Since the disaster, AEU-UEM has been able to deliver 14 tonnes of relief aid.

Critical Aid Items

  • Hygiene Materials
  • Medications
  • Clothing
  • Food

We encourage all entities which are able, to please forward aid items directly to the forwarding address below. If an entitity is unable to forward aid directly and needs assistance Africa for Mozambique can assist in this regard. With all of the above considered, we prefer that donors make cash donations. This is because we prefer to avoid the logistical cost of getting the goods to Maputo, where they are shipped to disaster areas. Maputo has enough stock for UAE-UEM to purchase the aid items within Mozambique and forward to disaster areas.
